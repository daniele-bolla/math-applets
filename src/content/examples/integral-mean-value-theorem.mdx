# Meanvaluetheoremforintegrals

<MathBlock type="theorem" title="Meanvaluetheoremforintegrals">
Let $f : [a,b] \to \mathbb{R}$ be continuous.  
Then there exists a $\xi \in [a,b]$ such that

$$
\int_a^b f(x)\, dx = f(\xi)\,(b - a).
$$
</MathBlock>
