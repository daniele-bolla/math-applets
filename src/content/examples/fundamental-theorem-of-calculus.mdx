# FundamentalTheoremOfCalculs

<MathBlock type="definition" title="Taylor polynomial, remainder term">
Let $I \subseteq \mathbb{R}$ be an interval, $f : I \to \mathbb{R}$ be continuous, and $a \in I$.  
Then the following statements are satisfied:

i) The function
$$
F : I \to \mathbb{R}, \qquad
x \mapsto F(x) := \int_a^x f(t)\,dt
$$
is an antiderivative of $f$.
</MathBlock>
